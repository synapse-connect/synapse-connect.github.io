Why Synapse

Verified credentials

Every profile is verified against state licensing boards and the NPI registry. No catfishing, no "I work in healthcare" hand-waving — just confirmed clinicians and care teams.

Schedule-aware matching

Share your call schedule and off-days. Synapse surfaces matches whose free time actually overlaps with yours — so the first date isn't three weeks out.

Built for shift workers

AI-suggested date times based on both schedules. Curated date spot suggestions near a midpoint. A community that knows what "post-call" means.

How it works

  1. 1

    Verify once

    Upload your government ID and your healthcare license. AWS Rekognition matches the photo to a selfie; our team confirms credentials against state board records.

  2. 2

    Set your schedule

    Mark your typical on-call days and off-shifts. Synapse uses this to prioritize matches whose availability overlaps with yours.

  3. 3

    Match and meet

    When there's a mutual signal, you'll get AI-suggested date times and venue ideas near a comfortable midpoint. From there, it's your call.

About Synapse

Synapse is built by a small team with first-hand experience inside the healthcare system. We saw friends and partners struggle with dating apps that didn't account for 12-hour shifts, rotating call, or the verification gap that lets non-clinicians claim a credential they don't hold. We're building the dating app we wished existed during residency.
